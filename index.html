Nigeria’s Future Won’t Be Built on Oil. It Will Be Built on Data.

We are empowering today’s students with the data skills they need to thrive in tomorrow’s world. Join us as we teach data literacy to the next generation of Nigerian leaders.

Problem

Nigeria’s future leaders are growing up in a world where data is the new language of opportunity. Yet, our public schools are struggling to equip students with the skills they need to succeed in a global economy driven by data. Less than 10% of Nigerian secondary school students are introduced to data analysis, and even fewer have access to the technology and mentorship needed to master it.

Solution

That’s where Data for NextGen Nigeria comes in. We are dedicated to bridging this gap by providing hands-on, practical data education to students in underserved communities. Our workshops, resources, and mentorship programs teach students to think critically, analyze data, and use tools like Excel and Python to solve real-world problems. We are preparing today’s students for tomorrow’s jobs.

Vision

We envision a future where every Nigerian student is empowered with the tools, confidence, and skills to compete on the global stage.

Mission

Our mission is to provide data education to 10,000 Nigerian students within the next three years, ensuring that they are equipped with the skills to thrive in a data-driven economy.

The Power of Data Can Change Everything. Help Us Put It in the Hands of Every Student.

By learning how to work with data, students gain a tool that can open doors in countless industries—from finance to healthcare, technology to agriculture.

Data Is the Key to Unlocking Nigeria’s Next Chapter. Our Passion Is to Hand It Over to the Next Generation.

At Data for NextGen Nigeria, we’re not just educators—we’re advocates. We believe that every student in Nigeria deserves access to the skills that will shape the future.
